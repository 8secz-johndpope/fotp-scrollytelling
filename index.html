These are best countries to be a journalist

Note that only one country — Norway — has a press freedom score below 10 in the latest press freedom ranking. A lower score indicates higher press freedom. A country must score below 30 points for its press to be considered "Free".

G20 countries are losing points too

G20 countries are no exception to this trend of rising antipathy towards the press. In 2000, only two G20 countries were Not Free (a score higher than 60). By 2017, there were five.

The world is slowly inching towards authoritarianism
